12 Zodiac
Soda can packaging design
The client is a new Chinese soda company producing fruit-flavored drinks for a youth-oriented demographic
The combination of Chinese zodiac and Western painting style brings together Chinese culture and Western culture, which is actually the theme of this product. Each Zodiac soda represents an excellent blend of fruit flavors which allows you to have a taste of your own Zodiac while appreciating the art.
